# Google Fonts

Yes, you can use Google Fonts in TablissNG! Follow the steps below:

1. Find your favourite [Google Font](https://fonts.google.com/).
2. Press **Select this font** and open the embed pop-up at the bottom.
3. Under **Embed Fonts**, copy the `@import` embed line (not the `<style>` lines, just the `@import` line!).
4. In TablissNG, add the **Custom CSS** widget and paste the embed line in the text box.
5. Back in Google Fonts, copy the font name from the **Specify in CSS** section.
6. Back in TablissNG, paste the font name in the **Font** field under a widget's Font Settings.
7. Enjoy TablissNG in your font!
